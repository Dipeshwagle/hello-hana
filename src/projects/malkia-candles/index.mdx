description: Malkia Candles offers candles with a travel theme. Each scent connects to a specific place in the world. This website was designed by the team at <a href="https://www.pimprestondesigns.com/" target="_blank">Pim Preston Designs</a>, and built by myself. Some unique elements of this website include the colours and graphics unique to each product page to help link the candle to a specific scent and location. For example, each product page has a unique colour, passport stamp image noting the scent location, unique fragrance descriptions and unique imagery including maps and ‘polaroids’. Since all product pages follow the same template, unique product pages required a developer that specializes in Shopify development. This website was beautiful and I was excited to make the most of Shopify’s metafields feature.
